Latitude vs Humidity

Humidity seems to be slightly correlated with latitude in that it forms a "W" shape, but the correlation is weak.

The humidity chart shows some interesting trends. First, that around the equator, all of the sampled cities have a higher humidity. Cities with lower humidity tended to be 20 degrees away from the equator or more. As the data nears the poles, humidity tends to rise again.
